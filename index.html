Abstract

In this paper, we investigate the safe execution of robotic manipulators operating in dynamic environments. Safety in such scenarios requires enforcing collision-avoidance constraints under rapidly changing observations of moving obstacles. We present an online, perception-based neural control barrier function framework for real-time safety enforcement during manipulation.
